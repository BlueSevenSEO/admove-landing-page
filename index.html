Your Strategy Questions, Answered

What kind of data does the AI analyze for strategy?

Our AI can analyze data from various sources you connect, including your Meta/TikTok ad accounts (historical performance), Google Analytics (website behavior), and your CRM (customer data). It uses this to understand what has worked in the past and identify your most valuable audiences.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) for all data in transit and at rest. Your data is yours alone and is never used to train models for other customers. We are fully SOC 2 compliant.

Can I edit the strategy the AI proposes?

Yes. The AI provides a comprehensive blueprint, but you always have the final say. You can review the recommendations, remove audiences you don't want to target, adjust budget suggestions, and refine creative angles before moving to the activation stage.
